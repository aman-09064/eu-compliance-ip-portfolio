# Safety Data Sheet (SDS) Compliance Checklist

## 1. Purpose of This Checklist

This checklist is used to:
- Verify completeness and correctness of Safety Data Sheets (SDS)
- Assess compliance with EU REACH and CLP requirements
- Support internal audits, supplier qualification, and inspections
- Demonstrate due diligence in chemical safety communication

This checklist applies to **substances and mixtures** placed on the EU market.

---

| Check | Yes / No | Notes |
