# Supplier REACH Compliance Questionnaire

## 1. Purpose of This Questionnaire

This questionnaire is used to:
- Assess supplier compliance with EU REACH obligations
- Identify regulatory risks in the supply chain
- Support due diligence, audits, and inspections
- Ensure accurate information flow for SDS, SVHC, and SCIP compliance

All suppliers providing substances, mixtures, or articles for the EU market are expected to respond.
